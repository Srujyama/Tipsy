Common Questions

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → Delete Account. Your data is removed immediately.

How accurate is the BAC reading?
Estimates only. BAC depends on body chemistry, food, medication, and other factors no app can measure exactly. Never drive under the influence.

I forgot my password.
Tap "Forgot password?" on the sign-in screen. You'll get a reset link by email.

How do I add friends?
Open the Social tab and search by username.
